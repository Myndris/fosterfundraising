Accidental Foster Kittens

Things did not go exactly as planned when we got back from pride weekend. We recently discovered a mama cat had a litter of newborns inside our floorboards. We set out traps to grab her before touching the kittens, but it turns out she sneakily moved the babies right after we spotted them! However, while we had traps set for her, we ended up catching two kittens from a completely different neighborhood litter.

I am glad we could at least get two kittens indoors and safe to help manage the local exploding population. They are doing wonderfully and settling in. There are no emergencies, just some simple gunky eyes that need clearing up.
